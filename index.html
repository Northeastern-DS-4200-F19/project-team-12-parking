Motivation

The Chester Square neighborhood has to deal with a lack of adequate parking spots. Additionally, the parking spots that do currently exist are not necessarily being utilized in the best way. We have created these visualizations in order to help the community find easier access to parking in the Chester Square area. The intent of these visualizations is to guide changes to parking policies and parking utilization of the area.
